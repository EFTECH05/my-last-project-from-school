LIFE & COFFEE

Eskobar is Portuguese for ‘life and coffee’. It’s really that simple – no coffee, no life; no life, no coffee. Inspired by the street cafès of Portugal, infused with the lively energy of the people of Africa, and blended together in a place that inspires the soul, our coffee is the embodiment of the vida way of life.

About Our shop

Coffee distribution

From our humble beginnings on Kloof St, we’re proud to have over 320 stores (and growing) on the African continent. Visit any of our locations across South Africa, Zambia, Ghana, or Mauritius and discover why Vida is the perfect spot to unwind, socialize, and indulge in life & coffee. We’re constantly on the lookout for new locations to join our dynamic family. Find out more today and join the largest and fastest growing privately-owned speciality coffee franchise in Africa.
