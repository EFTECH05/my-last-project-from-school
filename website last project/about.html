About Our shop

THE STORY OF OUR CAFÉ

Inspired by our home blend, Estrela, we’ve named our blends after iconic celestial bodies. Like the brightest stars that adorn the night sky, our Constellation of Coffee blends are instantly recognizable and timeless in their charisma. We’re committed to our on-going “vida e mundo” (life & the world) journey to ensuring a sustainable world for everyone & everything living on our beautiful planet. Let’s be better together!
